Final chapter Bring the three scales together 05 →

Synthesis

There is no single number for a better diet.

A food choice connects national production, environmental cost, and nutritional function. Cheese makes that connection visible: a familiar product can carry a concentrated burden, while any substitute must still meet the body's needs.

The informed choice is not simply “less” or “more.” It is a choice made with the whole system in view.
