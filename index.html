Cheese & Ecological Cost

Creating dairy products requires a staggering amount of resources. To produce just 1 kg of hard cheese, Austrian dairies process roughly 10 liters of raw milk, leaving behind 9 liters of liquid whey and water loss.

While this physical mass balance looks straightforward, the true environmental toll remains hidden beneath the surface.
